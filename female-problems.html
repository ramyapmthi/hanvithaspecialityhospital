All Female Problems

A safe, judgment-free space for women's reproductive and general health care.

Treatment Overview

At Hanvitha Speciality Hospital, our Gynecology department led by Dr. Tejaswini addresses a wide spectrum of functional, hormonal, and anatomical female health issues. We are dedicated to delivering proactive diagnostics, empowering women of all ages to maintain excellent reproductive health.

Conditions We Treat

We provide medical intervention for:

  • Menopause & Perimenopause symptom management
  • Uterine Fibroids and Endometriosis
  • Urinary Tract Infections (UTIs) & Pelvic Inflammatory Disease (PID)
  • Abnormal vaginal discharge and infections
  • Primary and Secondary Infertility issues

Treatment Benefits

Consulting securely about these problems allows you to:

  • Detect dangerous cervical or uterine anomalies early
  • Solve ongoing localized discomfort quickly
  • Eliminate hormonal distress linked to menopause
  • Resume a healthy sexual and active life

Why Choose Hanvitha Hospital?

Many women delay treating intimate issues out of discomfort. We provide extreme confidentiality, a comforting female-led environment, and thorough testing protocols. You will be heard, respected, and treated with standard, up-to-date gynecological science.
