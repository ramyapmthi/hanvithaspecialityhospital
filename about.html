Hanvitha Speciality Hospital is a patient-focused multi-speciality healthcare centre delivering modern orthopedic surgery, spine care, gynecology, and maternity services to Sullurupeta and surrounding regions.

Our hospital combines advanced medical expertise with compassionate care to ensure every single patient feels safe, respected, and completely confident during their treatment journey.

We are recognized across the community specifically for our rapid emergency response, highly accurate diagnostic capabilities, and personalized, ethical treatment plans.

Our Mission

To deliver accessible, ethical, and advanced healthcare with deep compassion. We are dedicated to restoring health using evidence-based medical science and modern technology.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted emergency and speciality hospital in the region, recognized relentlessly for impeccable patient safety, fast recovery rates, and comprehensive care.
