Normal Delivery Care

Natural childbirth support with safe, continuous fetal monitoring.

Treatment Overview

At Hanvitha Speciality Hospital, we fundamentally believe in supporting natural, vaginal childbirth whenever medically feasible. Our specialized maternity wing is equipped to provide a soothing, empowering environment while our birth team continually monitors the critical vitals of both mother and baby.

Our Prenatal Support Approach

Our commitment to natural childbirth includes:

  • Antenatal counseling and diet instruction
  • Regular fetal ultrasound monitoring
  • Labour pain management protocols
  • Ready-to-go emergency backup (Neonatal and Surgical ICU)

Treatment Benefits

The benefits of progressing with a normal delivery under expert guidance lie in:

  • Significantly faster postpartum recovery
  • Shorter hospital stay length
  • Avoidance of major abdominal surgical scarring
  • Natural transition and immunity boost for the infant

Why Choose Hanvitha Hospital?

Dr. Tejaswini leads a caring team known for compassion and clinical strictness. We do not rush deliveries; we respect the natural process while implementing Metro-level safety precautions. The 24/7 availability of top-tier care means that you are in the safest hands in Sullurupeta for your big day.
