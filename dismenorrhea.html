Dismenorrhae Treatment

Advanced pain relief and medical management for severe menstrual cramps.

Treatment Overview

Dysmenorrhea refers to severe, debilitating menstrual cramps and pelvic pain. It is not something you merely have to "live with." Our specialists identify whether the pain is Primary (no underlying disease) or Secondary (caused by endometriosis, fibroids, etc.) and treat it accordingly.

Common Indications

It is important to seek medical care if your menstrual pain involves:

  • Cramping so severe it stops you from performing daily tasks
  • Pain escalating to the lower back and legs
  • Nausea, vomiting, or dizziness during periods
  • Pain that worsens as years pass

Treatment Benefits

Our treatment options provide relief and restore your routine:

  • Immediate blocking of pain-signaling hormones (prostaglandins)
  • Detection and shrinking of underlying fibroids
  • Improvement in overall chronic pelvic comfort
  • Emotional relief from recurring monthly trauma
