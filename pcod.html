PCOD Management

Modern diagnosis and hormonal balance therapy for PCOD patients.

Treatment Overview

Polycystic Ovarian Disease (PCOD) is a condition where the ovaries release many immature or partially mature eggs which eventually turn into cysts. We offer a structured diagnostic process, incorporating pelvic ultrasounds and blood tests, followed by customized treatment to normalize ovarian function.

Common Indications

If you have any of the following, consulting an expert is highly advised:

  • Scanty or missed periods
  • Heavy bleeding during periods
  • Pain in the pelvic region
  • Mood swings and depression

Treatment Benefits

Effective PCOD management leads to:

  • Normal, predictable menstruation
  • Prevention from turning into full-blown PCOS
  • Better management of cholesterol and lipids
  • Greatly improved odds for conception
