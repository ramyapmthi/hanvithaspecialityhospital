Spine Surgery

Fellowship-trained surgical care for severe back pain and disc issues.

Treatment Overview

Our dedicated Spine Centre offers comprehensive surgical and non-surgical solutions. Under Dr. Hariprakash Lokka's fellowship-trained expertise, we conduct highly complex procedures, ranging from microdiscectomy and spinal fusions to treating spinal deformities safely.

Common Indications

We treat a multitude of spine-related complications:

  • Herniated / Slipped Discs
  • Spinal Stenosis (Narrowing)
  • Degenerative Disc Disease
  • Sciatica (Shooting leg pain)
  • Spinal Fractures / Trauma

Treatment Benefits

Proper spine intervention delivers essential advantages:

  • Relief from severe nerve compression
  • Restoration of posture and stability
  • Avoidance of permanent paralysis
  • Reclaiming a functional daily routine

Why Choose Hanvitha Hospital?

Operating explicitly with high-definition microscopes and intra-operative monitoring safeguards, our spine procedures are Metro-standard. Our priority is avoiding nerve damage and ensuring a complete physical recovery with focused post-operative physiotherapy.
