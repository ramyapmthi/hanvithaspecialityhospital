Sports Injuries

Targeted rehabilitation and repair for athletes and active individuals.

Treatment Overview

Hanvitha Speciality Hospital provides dedicated clinical interventions for sports and physical strain injuries. From ACL/PCL tears to meniscus repairs and tendon issues, we utilize minimally invasive arthroscopic techniques to treat the core problem.

Common Indications

You may need sports injury treatment for:

  • Anterior cruciate ligament (ACL) tears
  • Ankle Sprains and muscle strains
  • Rotator cuff damage
  • Achilles tendon injuries

Treatment Benefits

Arthroscopic and localized sports treatments result in:

  • Minimal scarring and less pain
  • Returning to sports much faster
  • Restoring original tendon tensile strength
  • Halting chronic joint damage

Why Choose Hanvitha Hospital?

Our modern keyhole surgical capabilities and dedicated sports injury pathways ensure that an active lifestyle is returned to patients efficiently. Sourced from high-quality clinical protocols, our methods provide a low recurrence rate.
