High Risk Pregnancy

Specialized monitoring to protect mother and baby in complex pregnancies.

Treatment Overview

Not all pregnancies proceed normally. A high-risk pregnancy means maternal or fetal complications have a higher chance of occurring. Our intensive care maternity wing is designed to tackle these complications head-on, deploying advanced medical interventions to ensure a safe delivery.

Clinical Risk Factors

Pregnancies are considered high-risk under conditions such as:

  • Advanced Maternal Age (over 35 years)
  • Pre-existing medical conditions (Diabetes, Hypertension)
  • Gestational Diabetes & Preeclampsia
  • Multiple gestations (Twins, Triplets)
  • History of previous complicated pregnancies

Treatment Benefits

By registering under our specialized care scheme, you receive:

  • Constant fetal growth monitoring via advanced scans
  • Aggressive blood pressure and blood sugar management
  • Prophylactic treatments to prevent premature birth
  • Round-the-clock surgeon access for emergency interventions
