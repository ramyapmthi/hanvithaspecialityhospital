All Trauma Care

Immediate and expert orthopedic trauma management when seconds count.

Treatment Overview

Our trauma care unit at Hanvitha Speciality Hospital is equipped to handle complex skeletal injuries, road traffic accidents, and severe fractures. We operate 24/7 under the leadership of Dr. Hariprakash Lokka, providing rapid diagnosis and precise stabilization.

Common Symptoms & Indications

Seek immediate trauma care if you experience:

  • Visible bone deformities or fractures
  • Extreme, uncontrollable joint pain
  • Severe swelling and rapid bruising
  • Inability to bear any weight

Treatment Benefits

Our comprehensive emergency response ensures:

  • Minimal tissue damage
  • Faster recovery pathways
  • Reduced risk of future deformities
  • Preventive infection care
