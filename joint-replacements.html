Joint Replacements

Advanced knee, hip, and shoulder replacement procedures for a pain-free life.

Treatment Overview

Hanvitha Speciality Hospital provides advanced Joint Replacement Surgery (Arthroplasty). Led by Dr. Hariprakash Lokka, we use minimally invasive techniques and highly durable implants to restore function to knees, hips, and shoulders affected by severe arthritis or injury.

Common Symptoms indicating surgery

You may need joint replacement if you face:

  • Chronic joint pain unresolved by medication
  • Stiffness affecting daily mobility
  • Joint swelling that does not improve
  • Difficulty climbing stairs or walking

Treatment Benefits

Total and partial joint replacements offer excellent outcomes:

  • Complete pain relief
  • Restored natural movement radius
  • Increased quality of active life
  • Durable solutions lasting up to 20 years

Why Choose Hanvitha Hospital?

With an Ex-Apollo consultant leading the procedures, we follow international protocols for infection control and rapid recovery. We ensure post-surgical rehabilitation and physiotherapy are perfectly synchronized to let you walk comfortably within days.
